## About

This application, built for internal use of the **T.A. Pai Institute of Management, Bengaluru**, aimed to streamline internal support operations and communication between the students, faculty and administration of the institute. Led by Raghav Gupta, the team developed an end-to-end portal with a complete system architecture, database design, role-based access control (RBAC) and dynamic user interfaces and API responses.

The HelpDesk System consisted of three different roles – users, resolvers, and administrators. These roles could take pre-defined actions on different levels, with overrides that were implemented as per the stakeholder requirements. Each ticket could be assigned a severity, SLA, as well as categories and subcategories. The resolvers could filter tickets by any of these attributes, and administrators could manage and create new attributes on the application-level.

This application helped us learn to manage evolving requirements, craft organized demonstrations, and to build systems that elegantly co-operate with other constituents of the client's environment.
