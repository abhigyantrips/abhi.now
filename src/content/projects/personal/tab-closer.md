## About

It's a browser extension that closes tabs. That's it dude.

## The Story

I'd always been curious about how browser extensions worked. The idea that you could add a "module" to your browser and it can just _do_ things on websites you visit, was hard to wrap my head around.

This was around the same time that news was circulating about [uBlock Origin, the Ad Blocker, having to leave Google Chrome due to "manifest changes"](https://www.tomsguide.com/computing/software/google-is-disabling-one-of-the-worlds-biggest-ad-blockers-what-you-need-to-know). It made me wonder how a browser could just leave extensions behind through a software update. This would lead me to discover the big fight against corporate tech, but that was yet to come.
