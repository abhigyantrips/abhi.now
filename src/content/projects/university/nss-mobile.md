## About

This application was built for the National Service Scheme Student Chapter of MIT-BLR, with the aim to build a platform for NSS volunteers to log their contribution hours and apply to volunteer for ongoing events. This project was undertaken by our HackNXS hackathon team "SKARR", when we secured the 3rd position in the hackathon and were requested by NSS MIT-BLR to carry out the development.

We worked on creating a centralized platform for all volunteers that was easy to onboard and access. with the ability to access faculty information, the NSS repository, events and volunteer hours all from one app, it cleared up the time previously spent on clerical work/scheduling and opened up the core committee to spend more time doing what they do best, which is bringing smiles to people's faces.

This project is a good proof-of-concept for volunteer organizations, and has been kept open-source for other students to learn and explore.

One lesson we learned on this journey was to optimize costs and relay the same to the stakeholders quickly, as hosting APIs can be expensive, and relaying the quote to NSS they rightly said, "it would be better for us to spend that money on social service."
