## About

This application aimed to create an end-to-end portal that handles entry and storage of overlapping data parameters from different institutional accreditations, and exports data based on the accreditation selected by the user.

This was a highly data-centric project, and required multiple months spent specifically on breaking down different accreditation parameters and finding commonalities for storage. The database schema was also transformed multiple times with changing stakeholder requirements and long-term scope of the project. In the end, we produced a dynamic and highly extensible model that could be worked upon by future teams of the Application Development Cell.

Led and managed by Abhigyan Tripathi, the team delegated the tasks of parameter breakdown and project modules equally. With Nived Mohan spearheading the data modelling and Hari Keshav Rajesh prioritizing SQL query composition, the database schema was formed in an elegant manner. The development of the backend component was led by Raghav Gupta, who also managed the deployment of the project on the local machine provided by the institute. With the development of the backend systems complete, Abhigyan Tripathi designed and developed the frontend portal that would allow users to interact with the data. Satyajitsinh Jhala engaged in a multi-dimensional role by working on the data modelling as well as query composition. Rishabh Yadav established the end-to-end workflow for exporting data to PDFs, while Sohan Venkatesh documented all the complex processes taking place on the portal simultaneously.

This was a large-scale project, and will evolve rapidly with changing accreditation parameters as well as the requirements of educational institutes.
